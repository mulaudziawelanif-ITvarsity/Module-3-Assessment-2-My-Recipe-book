LAMB SHANK

Prep time: 90 mins

Yied: 5 ½ dozen

Ingredients














Yoghurt garlic sauce:







Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 170°C.
  2. Season the lamb shanks with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Heat the oil and brown the shanks all over.
  4. OAdd the garlic, carrots, celery and onions and fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Transfer to an ovenproof casserole dish. Mix the buttermilk, tomatoes, stock and flour together.
  6. Pour over the lamb shanks. Add the rosemary.
  7. Cover and bake for 2 – 3 hours until tender and falling from the bone.
  8. Check on the hour and baste with the sauce.
